Our Story

Built with love,
driven with purpose

We're a grassroots time attack team built around one of the greatest little sports cars ever made — a 1992 Mazda Miata that loves a good corner as much as we do.

Racing is our happy place. The track is where the noise of life fades out and it's just you, the car, and the road ahead. We don't chase championships — we chase good laps, good memories, and good people.

The name Littlefoot is a nod to two things: the lovable dinosaur from the movies, and a young man who left some very big footprints behind.

💛 Every lap we run carries a little extra meaning. This team exists because someone we loved showed us that even the smallest among us can leave the biggest marks. We race in his honor.
